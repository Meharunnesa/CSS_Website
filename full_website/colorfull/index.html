About Yunnan

Incredible Cultural Appeal

Yunnan is home to 26 of China’s 56
ethnic groups, including the Han
people.

Appealing all year round

The mild, springlike climate in
Kunming and surrounding areas
makes it an appealing destination all
year round.

Geographically Distinctive

Sitting on the border of Myanmar,
Laos and Vietnam, Yunnan is the only
province in China to link South Asia and Southeast Asia.

Amazing Natural Scenery

Yunnan boasts diversified landscapes
from snow-capped mountains,
brilliant lakes, plunging gorges to
tropical rainforests.
